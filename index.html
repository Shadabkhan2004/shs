With over 30 years of experience across India and the MENA region, SSH Industrial Equipment and Allied Services is a multi-disciplinary industrial supply and engineering solutions company headquartered in Hanamkonda, Telangana, India.

We serve EPC Contractors, Engineering Consultants, and End-Users across some of the most demanding industrial environments in India and the GCC region — delivering the right equipment, at the right time, every time.

Our team brings deep expertise in sourcing, logistics, and on-site support, enabling clients to focus on operations while we manage supply chain complexity.
